But when one looksclosely he sees that in the elaborate and complex design along one of the pillars,one of the small design elements is carved upside down; otherwise the thing iscompletely symmetrical. If one asks why this is, the story is that it was carvedupside down so that the gods will not be jealous of the perfection of man. Sothey purposely put an error in there, so that the gods would not be jealous andget angry with human beings.
